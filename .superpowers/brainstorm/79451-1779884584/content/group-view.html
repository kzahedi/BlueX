All accounts on one chart as separate coloured lines. Easy to spot which outlet attracts the most hate, and how trends move together or diverge. Metric tabs switch the chart between Hate %, Counter %, and Volume. German media in warm colours, US in cool/dashed — visually separates the groups.

B — Small Multiples Grid

Each account gets its own mini-chart, all at the same Y-axis scale. Hate (solid) and counter (dashed) both visible per account. Avg hate % shown as a badge. Easier to read each account individually — harder to see precise cross-account comparisons but less visual clutter.

The two views can co-exist — a toggle between "Overlaid" and "Small Multiples" in the group view. Pick your default preference.
